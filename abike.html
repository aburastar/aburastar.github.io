Love outdoor cycling / running / hiking? Try ADD-BIKE!


ADD-BIKE is a full featured APP to monitor your cycling (or running / walking / hiking) activities. It shows and track your activities in real time!

Best yet, you can use it as a cool speedometer and a full featured trip computer for your car / motorcycle!



Download (or Search Shihwei Yu from the main Google Play Store and select ADD-111R or ADD-PRO / ADD-LITE then look for "more by Shihwei Yu")






Showing current speed, max. speed, average speed, time/date, compass, calories burned, current elevation, min. / max. elevation 

Realtime display with clear numbers, high contrast graphics, indications, and audioable beep confiramtions

Realtime route info on Google map display

Fully controlable trip operations - start / pause / resume / complete / save / new functions  

Full featured trip data and route map can be saved for vewing / comparsion for all outdoor activities
Convenient toggling to keep screen ON for CAR / Motocycle (use as a fancy speedometer / trip computer)
Selectable ft-mile-mph vs m-km-km/h for your convenience

No fussy menu - just touch to select your activity mode and start your activity immediately
