Quick-Start Guide
  1. 1. Launch the ART app; read the safty note carefully and click OK
  2. 2. A quick help / tips dialog shows up, click OK to start using the ART app
  3. 3. Touch the center of the G-force meter to calibrate the G-sensor and compensate your phone position
  4. 4. App automatically accquiring GPS signal with indicator shown in RED (searching), Orange (poor signal), Green (good signal)
  5. 5. App will show your location, driving info once GPS signal is acquired (time required varies depend on your device)
  6. 6. Touch the "Start" button to start recording your road trip. Timing clock, route info, speed...all data will now start recording
  7. 7. Touch the "Pause" button to pause the trip info recording - the buttons now change to " Resume" and "Done"
  8. 8. Touch the "Resume" button if you want to resume the trip recording OR touch "Done" button to stop trip recording
  9. 9. If you touch the "Done" button - the buttons chnages to "New" and "Save"
  10. 10. Touch the "Save" button to save your trip record OR touch "New" to re-start a new trip
  11. 11. Swipe left / right to change modes (0-60 mph testing; 0-1/4 mile testing; Records history; Normal)
  12. 12. For 0-60 mph and 0-1/4 mile testing, you must fully stop with speed showing "0" to activate the test
  13. 13. Touch "Test" button to activate the 0-60 mph and/or 0-1/4 mile testing
  14. 14. A blinking "Go" to remind you the testing is ready - all you need to do is drive off and the test will start automatically
  15. 15. If test is successful (reach 60mph and/or 1/4 mile without speed drops to zero OR swipe out to other mode...) - result will be displayed
  16. 16. if test is incomplete, it resets to standby mode (showing current speed) with "Test" button displayed
  17. 17. To see the saved trip records, swipe to Record History mode. Swipe up / down to browse the saved records
