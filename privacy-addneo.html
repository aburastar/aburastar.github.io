PRIVACY POLICY MODEL FOR MOBILE
APPLICATIONS
This privacy policy governs your use of the software application ADD-NEO for mobile devices
that was created by Aburastar. The Application use the GPS location service to calculate / display
user’s outdoor activities including locations, moving speed, direction, time...geo location related
information.
What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?
User Provided Information
The Application does not ask any user information such as name, phone
number, or email.
Automatically Collected Information
The Application requires user to grant the GPS/Location service from
user’s phone device. Once user grant the permission, the application will
collect user location information dynamically to perform the application
functions.
Does the Application collect precise real time location information of
the device?
Yes. This application use GPS technology (or other similar technology)
to determine your current location in order to perform the application
functions. Your location information is only used for performing the
functions and will not be shared to other users outside of this
application.
If you choose to save your activities recorded by this application - the
saved records will contain your location information as well as your
activities attributes such as traveling speed, direction, calories
burned...etc. The saved records can be viewed form the device - it’s your
own responsibility to protect / limit the access to your mobile device and
this application.
Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by
the Application?
No. This application will not share any information with third party.
What are my opt-out rights?
You can stop all collection of information by the application and erase
saved records easily by uninstalling the application. Please use the
standard uninstall processes provided by your mobile device.
Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information
Your location data saved as your activities records will be retained
within the application. All data will be erased immediately once
application is removed (uninstalled). You can also delete specific saved
record.
Children
This application only collects location information of the device when it
is running. It will not determine the user age and perform any specific
function.
Security
Your location data collected and saved is stored in your mobile device.
Other people can view your records if they get access to your mobile
device. You are responsible to practice the best protection on your
mobile device such as locking and password protecting your mobile
device.
Changes

We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the
new Privacy Policy at https://aburastar.github.io/ privacy-addneo.pdf.
You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes,
as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
Your Consent
By using the application, you are consenting to our processing of your
information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by
us.
